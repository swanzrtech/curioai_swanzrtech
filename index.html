� WHAT IS CURIO AI?

Real-Time Intelligence Platform

Curio AI is a real-time monitoring and alert platform that distills global news, social media, blogs, and forums into concise, AI-powered summaries with sentiment tags. It ensures you never miss critical updates that drive decisions.

Real-Time Monitoring

24/7 scanning across thousands of global sources to ensure you never miss crucial information.

AI Summaries

Sentiment Analysis

Emotional context and impact assessment for every update, helping you understand the implications instantly.
